## Theoretical Foundation

### McGilchrst Truth-Knowledge-Action Triad (Chapters XXXVIII-XLII)

**Truth-as-disclosure:** Engagement quality emerges when operators feel tools respond to *what matters* rather than just *what can be measured*. Presence indicators create disclosure events — the system reveals itself at moment-of-need rather than hiding in repository structure.

**Knowledge-as-engagement:** async_prep's 23-day silence suggests information accumulation without operational closure. Intervention must create embodied familiarity through repeated, low-friction interaction opportunities.

**Art-making attention:** Broad field awareness + present-moment immersion ≠ narrow goal optimization. Quiet window prompts invite creative engagement rather than instrumental task completion.

### Right-Hemisphere Preservation Mechanisms

1. **Ambient channels:** Persistent browser indicator provides continuous reality grounding
2. **Novelty detection:** Reaction buttons allow immediate anomaly signaling
3. **Context injection:** Pre-written briefs frame questions within operator's lived experience
4. **Intentional asynchrony:** Operator engages on their cadence, not system's polling interval
